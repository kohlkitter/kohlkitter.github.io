transformation of lucy

interior

for the interior i ripped it all out took out all of the sound insulation and got rid of the heat ac and radio. i also stripped the rear to give it a clean street-able look.

exterior

for the exterior i have painted it almond in a satin finish. i rolled the fenders dropped it a good 5" all around. i tucked the wiring harness so the wheels would not rub through the harness in the front.
